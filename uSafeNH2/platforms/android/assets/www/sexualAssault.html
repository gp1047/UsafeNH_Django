Sexual Assault Policy

Under Title IX, all schools are required to have a notice of non-discrimination that prohibits discrimination on the basis of sex, as well as a policy that prohibits and addresses sexual assault. Learn more... Some schools address this in a policy that covers other forms of sexual violence or sex-based harassment. These policies are often titled “sexual misconduct,” “sexual harassment,” or “sex-based discrimination.” The policy must be widely disseminated. It is usually found within the student handbook and/or posted on your school’s website.
