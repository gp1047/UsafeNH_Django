Privacy Policy

uSafeNH is a confidential resource committed to protecting your privacy. The University of New Hampshire Prevention Innovations Research Center (PIRC) does not collect and will never transmit personal identifying information (“PII”), including but not limited to your name, telephone number, IP address, or location, to your college, university, or any third party. All information including phone numbers will remain on your personal device.


In order to evaluate our effectiveness and improve our services, we collect “anonymized and aggregated” data. Any and all information collected is stripped of PII, and only data related to feature use and functions within the uSafeNH app will be collected.


We do not collect


1. Names
2. Telephone numbers
3. GPS Coordinates


Information sharing: PIRC will not rent, sell, trade, or otherwise share PII with third parties.


External websites: uSafeNH contains links to external websites that we cannot guarantee are up-to-date, secure and/or confidential; use of those links are at your own risk, and we do not make any warranty or promise regarding those websites or their privacy practices.


Changes to this privacy policy and notification: We will update this policy if our data collection practices are changed. If we make any changes pertaining to our collection, retention, use or sharing of PII, we will make the update available upon login to the uSafeNH app. The most up-to-date version of this policy is located on our website http://www.usafenh.org


Contact information: Any questions about this privacy policy should be addressed to prevention.innovations@unh.edu


Effective Date: August 22, 2016
